Frequently asked questions

Before you schedule

What should I send for a quote?

Your location, a short project description, and any useful photos or product links. More detail helps us provide more accurate pricing.

How is pricing determined?

Starting prices are shown for common projects, but each project is reviewed based on the item, complexity, location, and information provided. You receive the price before work begins.

Are you insured?

Yes. Legacy Install & Assembly LLC carries general liability insurance. Proof of insurance is available upon request.

What if parts, hardware, or instructions are missing?

Please have the product, required hardware, and instructions available before the appointment. If replacement parts or hardware are needed, we may be able to help coordinate a store trip or pickup when agreed in advance. The cost of materials, store-trip time, and any additional travel or labor will be discussed and added to the invoice. A return visit may also involve an additional trip fee.

What does the included labor time mean?

Each starting price includes up to the stated amount of labor time for that project type. The final quote is confirmed before work begins.

What if my project takes longer than the included time?

Additional labor is $85 per hour. If more time is needed, we will discuss it with you whenever reasonably possible before continuing.

Is cleanup included?

Standard work-area cleanup is included when boxes and trash are left on the premises. Premium Packaging Removal & Disposal starts at $20 and includes vacuuming, box and trash cleanup, and haul-away. Final removal pricing depends on the amount and type of material.

How do cleanouts and dumpster coordination work?

Cleanout labor starts at $200 and includes up to 2 hours of labor. Dumpster coordination has a $50 coordination fee, plus the cost of the dumpster rental. We confirm the details and final pricing before work begins.

Is there a travel fee?

Yes. A $50 travel fee covers travel to the service location. Availability outside the immediate area depends on the project and distance.

Is Ohio sales tax included?

Yes. Ohio sales tax is included on all invoices as required by state law.

Do you perform electrical, plumbing, or major construction work?

No. We focus on assembly, setup, organization, and light installation—not licensed trades or structural construction.

Is the work covered by a warranty?

Yes. Workmanship errors caused by our installation are covered for 30 days, subject to the full workmanship warranty.

Do you travel outside Mingo Junction?

Yes, depending on the project and distance. Text your location and project details so availability can be confirmed.
